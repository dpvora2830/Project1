Area of Expertise

Protect your business from cyber risk

The growing prevalence and sophistication of modern cyberattacks means that organisations of every size now face more cyber risks than ever before. An experienced security services provider like Novi can prove to be an invaluable partner in mitigating risks to your business.

Backup and Recovery of critical systems

Today, any application or system downtime can significantly impact productivity and revenues. As well as ensuring you get back online quickly and seamlessly, a backup solution can protect you from sensitive data loss and serious reputational damage.

Professional IT Resources to assist your team

Businesses today are facing strong competition for IT talent with an evident skills gap in the market. As technologies and services such as cloud computing, virtualisation and effective cybersecurity become more complex, it can become unrealistic for small and medium-sized enterprises to acquire the in-house staff and knowledge to manage these areas.

Minimise operational risk and IT outages

The increasing volume and sophistication of cyber-attacks has left many small, medium and large enterprises at risk of data breaches. Organisations that take risks with their security can be left with long IT outages - disrupting operations and causing significant damage to your business and its reputation.

Modernise your IT systems

In the past, businesses were advised to embrace technology to gain a competitive advantage. Today, businesses that fail to put technology at the forefront of their business growth strategy will not survive. Novi can help you design and implement secure, modern and flexible technologies to increase productivity and support business growth.
